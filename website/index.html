Sprint Zero

Opinionated scaffolding for software project toolsets.

Every software project starts with a sprint zero.

Sprint zero is when your development team spend a couple of weeks choosing, installing and configuring tools. This upfront project "hit" will undoubtedly save time and money in the long term, but it's often painful when it doesn't need to be.

Sprint Zero makes opinionated choices on the tools that you use allowing your team to get on with the important stuff, delivering business value.

Let's get started.
